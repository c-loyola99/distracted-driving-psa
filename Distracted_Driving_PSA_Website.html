Distracted Driving Kills

Every day, people are injured or killed because someone looked at a text instead of the road.

Did You Know?

  • Over 3,000 people die each year from distracted driving in the U.S.
  • Texting while driving increases crash risk by 23x.
  • Looking at your phone for 5 seconds at 55 mph is like driving the length of a football field blind.

Real Stories

Jake, 17: “I lost my best friend because I glanced at a Snapchat.”

Maria, 33: “A driver didn’t see my daughter in the crosswalk. He was texting.”

How to Prevent Distracted Driving

  • Put your phone on Do Not Disturb while driving.
  • Use hands-free systems only when absolutely necessary.
  • Pull over to read or send texts.
  • Speak up if you see someone driving distracted.
